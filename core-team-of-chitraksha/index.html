Core Team 2022-23

The Media Cell of SGGSIET is a dynamic and dedicated team that is responsible for promoting and highlighting the achievements of the institution. The team is comprised of five different committees that work together to achieve their common goals.

At the helm of the team is Dr. Ganesh Pakle, the Dean of IT services at SGGSIET, who serves as the faculty coordinator. He brings a wealth of knowledge and experience to the table, ensuring the smooth functioning of the media cell.

The President of the team, Mr. Harsh Rathod, is the head of the team and the highest authority after the faculty coordinator. He oversees the overall direction and strategy of the cell and ensures that the team stays focused on its goals and objectives.

The Official Head, Mr. Vivek Deshmukh, oversees everything along with the president. He is responsible for the day-to-day operations of the media cell and ensures that the team works cohesively and that all tasks are completed in a timely and efficient manner. The Student Coordinator, Ms. Vanshita Totalu, reaches out to different clubs and acts as the bridge between the students and the rest of the team. She ensures that the students' needs and concerns are addressed.

The Chief Video Editor, Mr. Sainath Yeotikar, is responsible for creating and implementing all video projects. He is tasked with ensuring that the videos produced by the cell are visually appealing and informative.

The Photography Committee, headed by Mr. Aditya Deshmukh, is responsible for covering all events in the institution through lenses. The Videography Committee, headed by Mr. Kartikey Sakare, is in charge of creating and shooting cinematic and promotional videos for the institution. The Media Committee, headed by Ms. Sonia Hatkar, looks after all media-related activities and is the heart and soul of the team. The Publicity Committee, headed by Mr. Pramey Metkar, works with the Media Committee on the promotion of social media posts and videos of the team. The Finance Committee, headed by Mr. Abhishek Kannalwar, looks after bill-related matters, acquiring required equipment, and other matters for the team.

In conclusion, the Media Cell of SGGSIET is a dedicated and talented team that works together to achieve its common goals. With its five different committees, it is well equipped to carry out a range of projects and activities that aim to promote and highlight the achievements of the institution.

About Us

The Media Cell of Shri Guru Gobind Singhji Institute of Engineering and Technology (SGGSIET), Nanded is a platform that provides a creative and engaging environment for students to showcase their talents in multimedia and communication technologies. The cell is responsible for promoting and capturing the essence of the institute through various digital and traditional media channels such as photography, videography, web design, graphic design, and journalism.

At the Media Cell, students are trained in the latest technologies and techniques in multimedia production, and are encouraged to take an active role in shaping the institute's image and promoting its events and activities. The cell is well equipped with state-of-the-art tools and equipment, including high-end cameras, audio equipment, editing software, and more.

Throughout the year, the Media Cell organizes various events and competitions, including photography exhibitions, short film festivals, graphic design contests, and more. These events provide a platform for students to showcase their skills and compete with peers from other institutions. The cell also plays an important role in promoting the institute's achievements, such as awards, recognition, and other milestones, through various media channels.

The Media Cell of SGGSIET, Nanded is a dynamic and innovative space where students can develop their talents and interests in multimedia and communication technologies. Whether you're a photography enthusiast, a video production expert, or a graphic design pro, the Media Cell is the perfect place to hone your skills and make a positive impact on the institute.
