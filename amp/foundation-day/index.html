42nd Foundation Day

By Jyoti Bhosale December 28, 2022

17th of December 2022, Saturday, the NSS unit has arranged our Institute Foundation Day. It was the day to remember, cherish, and recognize the students. The illustrious 41 years have seen SGGS contributing to society by giving ethical engineers and research insights. The institute celebrated it with aplomb in the august presence of Sant Baba Sukhwinder Singh Ji, (Gurudwara Langar Sahib Nanded), Dr. P D Jadhav (Former I/C Principal), Prof. B M Naik (Former Principal), Prof. C B Deshpande(Former Principal), Dr. L M Waghmare (Former Director) & Dr. Y V Joshi (Director SGGSIE&T).

The theme for the 42nd year of our Institute is ‘|| तमसो मा ज्योततर्गमय ||’ presented by the NSS unit. The function started at 11:00 AM with a warm welcome of all honorable guests. Light symbolizes brightness and prosperity. It removes the forces of darkness and gives us positivity. In spiritual words, light means life. So, we can also think of light as giving us a new direction in life. With this noble thought, the function started with lightning of lamps & worshiping ‘Shri Guru Gobind Singh Ji’ by our guests.

Our Institute’s FSDC Club has performed Ganeshwandana & the cultural performance presented by Avaroh-The Musical Club. The program preface & the presentation on the history of our institute were represented by Dr. Mlind Bhalerao, Head of the ExTC Department.

After this felicitation of guest done by Dr. Y V Joshi, Director SGGSIE&T. 

The valuable guidance for students was given by Sant Baba Sukhwinder Singh Ji. Dr. P D Jadhav brought back the memories of institute history in his speech. Prof C B Deshpande & Prof B M Naik shared their experiences along with students. Dr. L M Waghmare shared his experience in the institute and gave the advice to utilize and earn the knowledge of technical facilities that are present. Dr. Y V Joshi has expressed his thoughts on the 42nd Institute Foundation Day of our institute. He appreciated the organizers and all the student volunteer committees for organizing this event.

Thereafter the felicitation of retired professor, teaching & non-teaching staff has been done by our guests along with our director sir. After all the guest lectures & felicitation of a retired professor, teaching & non-teaching staff speech was over, Dr. P G Jadhav expressed a vote of thanks.

It was the day to remember, cherish, recognize for the students. The function has 
concluded by singing our National Anthem.
