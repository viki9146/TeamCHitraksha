Zenith 2k23


After the grand success of Zenith'20 , Zenith'21 & Zenith'22 We are back with Zenith'23 - the 7th edition of our fiesta. This year the tagline is "Devour the Victory!!!". Last year this Sports extravaganza was scheduled in the month of February 2023 and was organised online .

There were many games that included Workout Wonders, NBA Live , Carrom Disc Pool, Free Style Football, Sports Quiz at intercollegiate levels and table tennis, chess, football, rink football, volleyball, kabaddi, cricket, basketball, handball, yoga,tug of war, ahtletics at intra-collegiate levels.

With the sport activities we conducted some social activites like orphanage activities, "run for health" (dream marathon). Prizes worth over 4.5 lakhs were awarded in the previous edition of ZENITH. The intercollegiate fiesta Zenith'20 , Zenith'21 & Zenith'22 competed among various Engineering colleges across the state.

The 8th edition of Zenith, the annual state level sports extravaganza organized by Shri Guru Gobind Singhji Institute of Engineering and Technology in Nanded, India, is coming soon with the tagline "Devour the Victory". This year's festival promises to be even more exciting and action-packed than previous years, with a wide range of indoor and outdoor sports events for students from colleges and universities across the state to participate in and showcase their athletic abilities.

Zenith is known for its well-organized events, exciting prizes, and its reputation as a hub for young athletes and sports enthusiasts. The festival provides a great platform for students to stay active, have fun, and develop important life skills such as teamwork, leadership, and communication.

This year's event is expected to draw a large number of participants, making it one of the largest and most anticipated sports festivals in the state. The festival will provide an opportunity for students to showcase their skills, challenge each other, and participate in a fun and competitive atmosphere.

The 8th edition of Zenith is shaping up to be an unforgettable experience, and the organizers are working hard to make sure that this year's festival will be even more memorable than previous years. Whether participating or spectating, Zenith promises to be a thrilling and exciting event that will be enjoyed by all involved. So come and join the fun, and let's Devour the Victory together!
