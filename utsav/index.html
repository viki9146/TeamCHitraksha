Utsav 2k23

UTSAV is a three-day cultural event organized by SGGSIET, Nanded. It is a platform for the students of the institute to showcase their talents and participate in various cultural activities. The event is managed by Team UTSAV, who are responsible for the successful execution of the entire program. This team comprises of dedicated students who work tirelessly to ensure that all the arrangements are made in a smooth and efficient manner.

During the UTSAV, students participate in a wide range of cultural activities such as singing, dancing, drama, and poetry recitation, to name a few. The event is also graced by the presence of renowned celebrities, who add to the excitement and glamour of the occasion. The celebrities interact with the students and encourage them to pursue their passions and interests.

Team UTSAV also plays an important role in conducting various cultural activities in the campus throughout the year. They are responsible for organizing workshops, competitions, and other events that help to foster a sense of community and creativity among the students.

In conclusion, UTSAV is an important event in the calendar of SGGSIET, Nanded, and serves as a platform for students to showcase their talents and interact with each other. The hard work and dedication of Team UTSAV helps to ensure that the event is a resounding success and that the students leave with cherished memories.
