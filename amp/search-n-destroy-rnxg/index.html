SEARCH N’ DESTROY (RNXG)

By Shruti Meshram February 6, 2023

Date : Saturday, 14 January 2023
Event Name : Search N’ Destroy (Line Follower Competition)
Rank : RUNNER UP TEAM
Venue : College of Engineering Pune (COEP)
Associated with : RNXG (Official Robo Club of SGGSIE&T,Nanded)

This BOT designed to follow a line or path already pre determined by the user. This line or path maybe as simple as physical black line on the white floor. 

The competition was set for January 14 (Saturday). Search N’ Destroy is a Line
Follower competition which was held at College of Engineering, Pune 

A team of five participants consisting of two second year students and three
first year students was successful.

List of participants-

Tasks assigned:
- Teams must build an autonomous robot which can trace the line.
- The path will have 4 checkpoints, 2 of which will be fixed in their positions
and the two will be variable checkpoints. It is mandatory to cross all the
checkpoints.
- The remaining two checkpoints (on the ground only) will be placed just
before the commencement of match (before dry run).
- The starting procedure of the bot must consist of a simple toggle switch to
start the movement. It must not involve giving the bot any manual force or
impulse in any direction.
- The machine should either stop or trace back after deposition of box in the
white space between the gap.
- Machine should not enter opponent’s area. Entering an opponent’s area has
a penalty of 50 points.

Team from Shri Guru Gobind Singhji Institute of Engineering and Technology.
and Being Part of RNXG Official Robo Club of SGGSIE&T
The prototype for competition was made by using components as below;
1) Arduino Nano (Microcontroller-Board)
2) RLS-08 (IR Sensor Module)
3) N20-6V-600 Rpm Micro Metal Gear Motor
4) Metal Ball Caster Wheel
5) L298N 2A Based Motor Driver Module
Since the first and second rounds of the competition team was in lead and
successfully covers all checkpoints in minimum time.

Conclusions:
The objective of the line following robot is to follow a line on its given path
which is obtained for which it uses IR sensor Array (RLS-08) which detects the
line and sends the information to Arduino Nano and then to L293N Motor
Driver which controls the working of the wheel.
COEP Mind spark 2023 gives great opportunity to participate and showcase our
programming and intellectual skills.
