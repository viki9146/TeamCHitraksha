74th Republic Day

Today, We Celebrated our 74th Republic Day at SGGSIE&T, Nanded. 73 years ago, we formally became a Republic and adopted our constitution on this very day. Republic Day fills each and every one of us with strong emotions. It brings to mind powerful images of India's military, parading to the world with its full might and grandeur. It inspires us with the moving accounts of the bravery and gallantry award winners.

On this glorious day, Team Drishti created a huge rangoli filled with vibrant colors that grabbed everyone's attention and became the highlight of the occasion. 

The event began with Flag Hosting by Our Hon. Director Sir and Chief guest of the day Shri Baba Sukhwinder Singh Ji, followed by salutes and marches by the guards. "Avaroh" the music club of the institute also graced the occasion with their mellifluent patriotic chores. 

The College chose this day to felicitate the sport achievement of the students in various tournaments. The college men's Football team, basketball team, chess champion boys; women's handball team, volleyball team, chess champion girls, and the most special our college cricket team that won the inter-college cricket tournament were felicitated.

A blood Donation camp was organized by NSS that received a remarkable response from college students. This was really a great initiative towards mankind and the nation to fill the droning blood banks.

A Street play was performed by "Gandharwa" the drama club of the institute.

The play "Kya Fark Padhta Hai" really took us into deep thought about the corruption, irrelevant politics, and cyber crimes prevailing in the country. It also questioned us about what role we play in stopping these crimes and supporting the nation.

Our institute has over 3000 students and so many faculties that come from different cultural, linguistic, social, religious, political, and ideological areas. Yes!! we are different but we are of the same kind!!

 

An effort by us to remark this day....🇮🇳
