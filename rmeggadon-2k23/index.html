RMAGEDDON 2k23 

Heard the song 'Woh Din' and how it just takes us back into our childhood days. So, team RNXG also tried to recreate that ambience again through Rmegaddon by keeping the theme 'Retro Gaming'.

DAY 1

The day started with inauguration of event by the hands of faculty coordinator Dr.M.V. Bhalerao in the presence of all the faculty members and revealing the theme for the very first game i.e. Retromania (Roborace).

Let me ask you a question, what do you think could be the theme?? ( Hint: Which video game was played by most people in their childhood??) Yes, it's Mario and the Retromania was based on it and the way arena was printed and obstacles were set up, it gave us the complete nostalgia!! The event was hosted well by the commentators Nikhilesh Deshmukh, Prasad Chede and Atharva Jagdale.

Some teams managed to cross all the obstacle that too within times and some unfortunately weren't able to complete it but you know learning is more important rather than winning. From all the teams, eight got into the semifinals, and as the event was by RNXG, they had a surprise for the contestants, some obstacles were added in the semifinals round to make the game more competitive.


And the whole event was covered by The Media Cell live.

DAY 2

The another was booked for the games Pac Runner (Line follower) having the the theme pacman and the Ninja Clash (Robo Sumo) based on the game Ninja Turtles.


For Pac runner, very few were able to complete it but the hardwork all the teams did for making it is so appreciative!!

For Ninja Clash various matches were held, and as it was a match of two, one team won and another team tried their best to won, everyone did so well, made the best bots possible..but at the end it's a game, so winner could only be one.


At the end there were quarter finals, semi finals and finals, the atmosphere was so energetic, everyone was hooting and cheering for the teams.
The event was concluded with the Prize Distribution Ceremony.


And winners were-

For Retromania
1) Team The Above All
2) Team Jaguar
3) Team Edge runners

For Pac runner
1) Team Thunder
2) Team Eagle Strike
3) Team Robusta

For Ninja Clash
1) Team Notion
2) Team DSUV
3) Team A2RP

Congratulations to all the winners and best of luck to all the contestants for their future!!
