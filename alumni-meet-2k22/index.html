Alumni Meet 2k22

Life has many phases and the best part of it is college life . after many years coming back ….. It is the time when we have flashbacks with good memories and bad though. In our prestigious college , Alumni meet 2022 was organized on 23rd and 24th of December 2022 by our faculty coordinator Dr M.V.Vaidya and the NSS club of our college. 

In this we had great fun and time to interact with them . All together there were three batches of the year : 1996, 1997 and 1998. The respected alumni had a great time enjoying these two days . The best part of the event was that they truly inspired us with their words.

 The event started at 09:00. They had breakfast at 09:00 am arranged in the food court . The media committee of team Chitraksha had amazing interaction with the Alumni . We had a questionnaire with them , and they shared their memorable and beautiful experience. The moments and their memories were so wonderful. Talking about their experience, it was that for nearly after 25 years they visited the college and they used to have great fun banter with each other during that time. 

They said that the college has major changes. And they shared how the college helped them to build and how they are successful in their life till now. They had a very good conversation with us. Their memories had so many emotions filled with love , immense joy and fun. And they said those three four years were the best part of their life and having fun with friends was one of the major and the best part. In their time they used to have a ‘ Dev mama canteen ‘ in the college and one tree hill point  that was the place where they used to hangout and chill with friends. The alumni shared their college memories, mentioning the changes in the college campus, facilities, and atmosphere. They were really impressed to see the infrastructure and greenery of the campus. Speaking about earlier facilities they told us that the labs and libraries were available 24x7 that helped them to learn and explore. This is one of the reasons that pushed them up in their life. When asked about the college events they mentioned the Khojagiri Poornima used to be celebrated above the rooftops of their departments overnight. They also mentioned about the earthquake that took place. 

At 10:00 am to 12:30 pm they had respective departments' interaction with faculty and students. It was a great meeting with them. And from 12:30 pm to 2 pm they had lunch organized in the food court. And from 2 pm to 5:30 pm we had a felicitation program of 1996 , 1997 and 1998 batch alumni in the main function hall . The event's chief guest was Mr. Abhijeet Raut, Hon. District collector. The event continued by our hon. director felicitating our alumni with a memorable frame. The alumni also introduced themselves and their work. They all were successful personalities, who expressed their desire to participate in and partner with various initiatives of the institute. An Alumni association of our college was launched "SEAA" i.e. SGGS Engineering Alumni Association.

They shared so much about their life after and before SGGS . We had a cultural program from 7 pm onwards. It was so much and they enjoyed it a lot . They danced and many of them were singing songs . It was great to listen to them and see them enjoying . Still in their 40s they had such great energy and enthusiasm. They had lunch and after that they had a bonfire and they enjoyed very well. On the next day 24 December from 09:00 am to 12:00 pm they had a sports bonanza along with breakfast on the sports ground. They had many events and literally they enjoyed each and every part of the event. And at last from 2:30 pm they had a visit to Kaleshwar temple and Gurudwara.

Overall it was a great event and we all had so much fun!!
