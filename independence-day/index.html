Independence Day

Warm greetings to all the Maharashtrians. In the following years, may this state progress with new advancements.
Maharashtra Divas is observed on May 1 by Maharashtra residents. This day marks the 62nd anniversary of the Bombay Reorganization Act, which established the state in 1960. Maharashtra Divas was commemorated at SGGSIE&T as every year, beginning with our director, Dr. Y. V. Joshi, hoisting the flag.
The event was attended by all of the institute's faculty members, as well as students and security officers.
The Constitution provided us with faith, liberty, and peace. So, let us take delight in this day.
“आम्हाला अभिमान आहे, महाराष्ट्रीय असल्याचा, आम्हाला गर्व आहे मराठी भाषेचा, आम्ही जपतो आमची संस्कृती, आमची निष्ठा, महाराष्ट्र दिनाच्या मनापासून शुभेच्छा… जय महाराष्ट्र”
