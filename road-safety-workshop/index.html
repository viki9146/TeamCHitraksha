Road Safety Workshop

A workshop for headmasters and sports teachers was organised on October 12 at Shri Guru Gobind Singhji Institute of Engineering and Technology on behalf of the Regional Transport Office and District School Bus Safety Committee. At this event, a street drama on driving safely was performed. A little over 300 instructors attended this programme.The street play featured students from Gnanmata English School and Nagarjuna Public School. Kumari Kstakshi Mahesh Chakrawar gave a speech regarding road safety.Dr Sunil Kulkarni gave a demonstration on First Life System (CPR). How to give life support to a person in case of unconsciousness or other causes of unconsciousness in an accident. To promote road safety, everyone in attendance took an oath. Deputy Regional Transport Officer Avinash Raut and Assistant Regional Transport Officer Sandeep Nimse gave guidance on road safety. Assistant Motor Vehicle inspector Vaibhav Dubbewar likewise planned the program and guided everyone regarding road safety.Rules 2011 guided the Principal.There is a District School Bus Committee at the district level for the safe transportation of students in the district. At the school level, there is a school transport committee. It was suggested that a school transport committee should be formed in all the schools and seating arrangements should be arranged as per the rules.The chief guest of the program was College Director Dr.Yashwant Joshi, Dr.Arun Kumar Patil, Rohit Das Basvate, Retired Principal Ashok Kamtane,Deputy Regional Transport Officer Avinash Raut, Assistant Regional Transport Officer Sandeep Nimse, Assistant Motor Vehicle inspector Vaibhav Dubbewar.
